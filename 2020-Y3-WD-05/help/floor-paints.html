Epoxy Floor Paints(Floor epoxy coatings)

There are many reasons why epoxy floor coatings are preferred over regular coatings for most commercial and factory floors.

  • Strength – An epoxy floor coating provides a much stronger and a durable film compared to any other regular floor paint, and as a result it provides a long lasting finish.
  • Resistance to Chemicals and Acid – Epoxy is resistant to chemicals and acids such as oil, bleach, gasoline, transmission fluid and more. Therefore, the coating does not discolour or stain easily.
  • Water Proof – non porous coating stops water or any other fluid to penetrate through the coating. Thus making it highly resistant to growth of moulds, mildews and fungus.
  • Less Maintenance – Due to its toughness, chemical resistivity and water proof properties, epoxy floors are much easier to clean and maintain. Thus reducing the maintenance cost rapidly.
  • Repairable – Despite its long lasting properties, once a repair is needed, it becomes easy to perform a repair without needing a complete renovation.

Application of an epoxy floor coating must be handled with care. Even though this is a tough coating, wrong preparation or conditions could fail the coating.

  • Surface Preparation: Surface preparation is an essential ingredient for a durable and a long lasting coat. Follow manufacturer recommended procedures for surface preparation.
  • Humidity: These solvent based epoxy systems bond weakly when humidity is present.
  • Substrate Surface: Epoxy coatings can only be applied over certain surface types, application is incompatible on surfaces such as wooden surfaces.
  • Wrong Ratio: Resin and Hardener must be mixed to right ratio, wrong proportions will lead to drying and strength issues.
